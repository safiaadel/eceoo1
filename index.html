What is Artificial Intelligence (AI) and how does it work?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a broad field of computer science that focuses on creating intelligent machines that can accomplish activities that would normally need human intelligence. Although AI is a multidisciplinary discipline with many methodologies, advances in machine learning and deep learning are causing a paradigm shift in nearly every sector of the IT industry.

History Of Artifical Intelligence:

Ancient Greek mythology included sentient robots and artificial entities for the first time. The creation of the syllogism and its application of deductive reasoning by Aristotle was a watershed point in humanity's search to comprehend its own intelligence.

It is begin from 1943 to 2016 and we will illustrate an abstract from 2005 to 2016 :-

2005 The DARPA Grand Challenge is won by STANLEY, a self-driving automobile. The US military is beginning to invest in self-driving robots such as Boston Dynamic's "Big Dog" and iRobot's "PackBot."
2008 Google makes advancements in speech recognition and incorporates it into its iPhone app.
2011 On Jeopardy!, IBM's Watson outperforms the competition.
2012 Andrew Ng, the Google Brain Deep Learning project's founder, feeds 10 million YouTube videos into a neural network using deep learning algorithms as a training set. The neural network learns to recognise a cat without being informed what a cat is, ushering in a new era of deep learning funding and neural networks.
2014 Google's self-driving car is the first to pass a state driving test.
2016 Lee Sedol, the world champion Go player, is defeated by Google DeepMind's AlphaGo. The ancient Chinese game's complexity was seen as a significant barrier to overcome in AI.

Artificial Intelligence Applications:

  1. AI in E-Commerce:

    Personalized Shopping

    Artificial Intelligence technology is used to create recommendation engines through which you can engage better with your customers.

    AI-powered Assistants

    Virtual shopping assistants and chatbots help improve the user experience while shopping online.

    Fraud Prevention

    AI can help reduce the possibility of credit card frauds taking place.

  2. AI in Navigation

     GPS technology can provide users with accurate, timely, and detailed information to improve safety. The technology uses a combination of Convolutional Neural Network and Graph Neural Network, which makes lives easier for users by automatically detecting the number of lanes and road types behind obstructions on the roads.

  3. AI in Robotic

     Robots powered by AI use real-time updates to sense obstacles in its path and pre-plan its journey instantly. It can be used for - Carrying goods in hospitals, factories, and warehouses Cleaning offices and large equipment Inventory management

  4. AI in Human Resource

    Artificial Intelligence helps with blind hiring. Using machine learning software, you can examine applications based on specific parameters. AI drive systems can scan job candidates' profiles, and resumes to provide recruiters an understanding of the talent pool they must choose from.

  5. AI in Healthcare

     AI is used in healthcare to build sophisticated machines that can detect diseases and identify cancer cells. AI can help analyze chronic conditions with lab and other medical data to ensure early diagnosis. AI uses the combination of historical data and medical intelligence for the discovery of new drugs.

  6. AI in Agriculture

     Artificial Intelligence is used to identify defects and nutrient deficiencies in the soil. This is done using computer vision, robotics, and machine learning, AI can analyze where weeds are growing. AI bots can help to harvest crops at a higher volume and faster pace than human laborers.

  7. AI in Gaming

     AI can be used to create smart, human-like NPCs to interact with the players.
